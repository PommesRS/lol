TIPS FOR TOURISTS IN AUSTRALIA


Australia is a great country for a holiday. But you have to be very careful there.
There are many dangerous animals: For example, there are snakes, spiders and jellyfish.
If you for stung by one of those you should visit a doctor.
The sun in the outback is also a very dangerous factor,
protect your skin with sunglasses, suncream, a sunhat/cap and a shirt.
In the water sometimes occur strong currents, to avoid
to get in one of these you should swim between the flags on the beach.
The outback is a very flat area, therefore there is the
"Royal Flying Doctor Service".
